Best Buy Anti-Scalping

Best Buy currently only offers pickup, so you'll need to physically go to the store and get it!

Best Buy Anti-Scalping Measures

Everyone is aware how difficult it is to obtain a PS5 and Xbox Series X right now. Due to scalpers and high demand, its extremely difficult to buy most high-end products nowadays. This guide will help you easily get one from Best Buy.

The PS5 and Xbox Series X currently retail for 499$ USD. Here is a currency converter to your local currency.

Best Buy drops every 2nd week on either Thursday or Tuesday, the day being random between the two. Drops are usually between 11am-4pm.

Credit to Dogix for this method!

  • Step 1 - Join the Best Buy Notifs discord server. Get the role for the product you want and wait. They ping the role when drops are incoming.
  • Step 2 - If not already using Chrome, download Chrome and set it as your default browser on drop days.
  • Step 3 - Set up your Best Buy account on Chrome AND Mobile App (linked Credit Card and other info, make sure your cart is empty).
  • Step 4 - Download the Stock Helper Extension.
  • Step 5 - Wait until the stock drops. This website can help you.
  • Step 6 - Go to the page for the product you want ASAP and click "Add To Cart".
  • Step 7 - Click "Get New Queue Time" in the extension until you get one under 3 minutes. Meanwhile, get the email client for your Best Buy account open.
  • Step 8 - While waiting for the queue timer to expire, open up your phone to the Best Buy app just in case.
  • Step 9 - Click on "Add To Cart" when the queue timer expires and make sure it adds to your cart.
  • Step 10 - Go to your cart and click "Checkout" on PC, if it doesnt let you into checkout, you have 2 options:
  • - Go to cart and attempt to checkout on mobile (try this one first, use #2 if it doesnt work).
  • - Repeatedly change to a different store and click "Checkout" on PC until it lets you through. If the item gets removed from your cart, restart from step 6.
  • Step 11 - Use your email to get the verification code (should be 7 digits).
  • Step 12 - Select the store you really want for pickup.
  • Step 13 - Enter your Credit Card security code and click place order. (You may be required to enter a Credit Card verification code from SMS, be ready to do that).
  • Step 14 - Hope you were quick enough!

Walmart Anti-Scalping

Walmart currently offers delivery, but it can take a few weeks!

If Best Buy isn't the best option for you, or you can't wait every 2 weeks, then Walmart is your best bet.

The PS5 and Xbox Series X currently retail for 499$ USD. Here is a currency converter to your local currency.

Walmart drops every week on either Thursday or Tuesday, the day being random between the two. Drops are usually between 11am-4pm. Unlike Best Buy, Walmart does waves of drops every 10 minutes, so it is significantly easier to get your product.

Credit to Dogix for this method also!

  • Step 1 - Join the Walmart Notifs discord server. Get the role for the product you want and wait. They ping the role when drops are incoming.
  • Step 2 - Go on the Walmart Mobile App and have your Walmart account set up (linked Credit Card and other info, Walmart APP, not website)
  • Step 3 - 2 seconds after the drop time, click the Walmart link from their Discord and have it open in the app. This website can help you with exact timing.
  • Step 4 - Click "Add To Cart" if it shows up. If it doesnt show up, go back to their Discord and click the link again.
  • Step 5 - Complete the verification steps. (Anti-Bot currently doesn't work on desktop website)
  • Step 6 - Checkout as fast as you can and hope you get it! If you don't, another drop will happen in exactly 10 minutes (usually theres 3 extra drops for a total of 4).
